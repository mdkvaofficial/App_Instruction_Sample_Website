Getting Started with HR-NotiWave

First of all, download the application from "Installation" on either of your Android or iOS device. Then, make an account to get started. Make sure you've read the features from "Features" before you proceed.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Sign Up and Log In: Create a new account by signing up with your email and setting a password. Once registered, log in to access the application.
  2. Explore Companies: Use the integrated chatbot to get information about various companies. This will help you understand the opportunities and requirements of each company.
  3. Create or Join a Company: If you're an employer, create a new company profile. As a candidate or employee, you can request to join a company of your choice.
  4. Mark and Track Attendance: Use the attendance feature to mark your presence daily. Employers can track attendance records through the app's intuitive dashboard.
  5. Request and Manage Leaves: Employees can submit leave requests through the application. Employers can review and approve leave requests based on company policies.
  6. Collaborate in the Chat Room: Engage with your colleagues using the chat room feature. This tool supports text, audio, and video communication to enhance collaboration.
  7. Manage Company Settings: Access the settings to manage your profile. You can leave or delete a company if necessary, providing flexibility in managing your career path.
