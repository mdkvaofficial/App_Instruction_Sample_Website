Core Features and Benefits

HR-NotiWave offers an array of features tailored to enhance the experience of every user within a company. One of the standout features is the intelligent chatbot, designed to assist candidates by providing valuable information about the company and guiding them through the application process. This feature alone sets HR-NotiWave apart from its competitors, offering a personalized touch that many other HR applications lack.

For job seekers, HR-NotiWave provides the ability to request to join a company of their choice. Employers, in turn, have the power to accept these requests based on their specific requirements and standards. This dynamic interaction not only simplifies the recruitment process but also ensures that companies attract the right talent.

Another key feature is the self-attendance marking system, which allows employees to easily record their attendance. This system is complemented by the leave application feature, enabling employees to request leave while allowing employers to evaluate and approve these requests based on the validity of the reasons provided. This comprehensive approach to attendance and leave management reduces administrative burdens and enhances transparency within the organization.

Teamwork and collaboration are critical components of any successful company, and HR-NotiWave addresses this need with an integrated communication system. By facilitating seamless communication within the application, HR-NotiWave eliminates the need for external communication tools, streamlining workflows and fostering a more connected workplace.
